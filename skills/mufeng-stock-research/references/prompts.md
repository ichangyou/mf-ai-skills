# Stock Research Prompts (Bilingual)

These are the 8 canonical research prompts. Substitute `[Company]` with the target company name.

Safety rule for every prompt: provide educational research only. Do not answer whether the user should buy, sell, hold, wait on, avoid, enter, exit, add, reduce, take profits, or treat a specific stock as investable. Do not issue price targets or action verdicts.

---

## English Prompts

1. **Fundamentals**
   Conduct a deep fundamental analysis of [Company], covering the latest financial statements (revenue, earnings, cash flow trends), competitive advantages (moat), and growth potential over the next 3–5 years.

2. **Downside Risk**
   Assess the downside risks for [Company], including market volatility exposure, key regulatory risks (antitrust, data privacy, sector-specific), and the impact of an economic recession or macro downturn on the business.

3. **DCF Scenario Analysis**
   Build an illustrative DCF (Discounted Cash Flow) scenario model for [Company]. Explicitly state all assumptions: revenue growth rate (by year), operating margin, WACC, terminal growth rate, and shares outstanding. Show the sensitivity table for ±1% changes in WACC and terminal growth. State clearly that the outputs are valuation scenarios for education, not price targets or investment-action instructions.

4. **Peer Benchmarking**
   Benchmark [Company] against its top 3–5 sector peers on key valuation multiples (P/E, Forward P/E, EV/EBITDA, P/S) and operational metrics (gross margin, operating margin, revenue growth, ROE). Explain how [Company]'s observed valuation compares with peers and what assumptions may explain any premium or discount, without implying an investment action.

5. **Upcoming Catalysts**
   Identify the top potential catalysts for [Company] over the next 6–12 months. Include product launches, earnings dates, M&A activity, regulatory approvals, macro/geopolitical events, and any analyst estimate revisions that could move the stock.

6. **Technical Analysis**
   Analyze [Company]'s price chart. Identify: current trend (uptrend/downtrend/sideways), key support levels, key resistance levels, volume signals, and any notable chart patterns (breakout, head & shoulders, double bottom, etc.). State specific price levels as market observations only, not entry, exit, stop-loss, or take-profit instructions.

7. **News Sentiment**
   Summarize the most significant recent news (last 30 days) about [Company]. Assess the overall market sentiment (positive/negative/mixed), and explain potential market reactions to the most important news items without predicting outcomes with certainty or recommending an action.

8. **Research Summary**
   Based on the fundamental analysis, risk assessment, DCF scenario analysis, peer comparison, catalyst outlook, technical picture, and sentiment above, synthesize the key findings for [Company] into a balanced research summary. Present: (1) Supportive Factors — the most important factors that could support stronger outcomes, with appropriate uncertainty caveats; (2) Risk Factors — the most important risks and headwinds; (3) Key Uncertainties — the 2–3 variables that most determine the outcome. Conclude with a brief, balanced synthesis paragraph. Do not issue a Buy/Hold/Sell rating, a price target, or any direct or indirect investment-action instruction. All conclusions must include language reflecting inherent uncertainty (e.g., "suggests", "indicates", "may", "based on current data").

## 中文提示词

1. **基本面分析**
   对[公司名]进行基本面深度分析，包括最新财务报表（营收、盈利、现金流趋势）、核心竞争优势（护城河），以及未来3至5年的成长潜力。

2. **下行风险评估**
   评估[公司名]的下行风险，涵盖市场波动敞口、关键监管风险（反垄断、数据隐私、行业监管）以及经济衰退或宏观下行对业务的影响。

3. **DCF情景分析**
   使用DCF（现金流折现）模型为[公司名]构建示意性估值情景，并详细说明所有假设：各年营收增长率、营业利润率、WACC（加权平均资本成本）、永续增长率及总股本。列出WACC和永续增长率各±1%变化下的敏感性分析表。明确说明输出是用于教育研究的估值情景，不是目标价，也不是投资动作建议。

4. **同行对标**
   将[公司名]与行业前3至5家主要竞争对手在核心估值倍数（市盈率P/E、远期市盈率、EV/EBITDA、市销率P/S）和运营指标（毛利率、营业利润率、营收增速、ROE）上进行对比，并说明[公司名]当前估值相对同行呈现溢价或折价的事实及可能原因，不得暗示任何投资动作。

5. **潜在催化剂**
   梳理[公司名]未来6至12个月的主要潜在催化剂，包括新产品发布、财报日期、并购动向、监管审批、宏观/地缘政治事件，以及可能影响股价的分析师预期调整。

6. **技术图表分析**
   分析[公司名]的价格走势图，识别：当前趋势方向（上行趋势/下行趋势/震荡）、关键支撑位、关键阻力位、成交量信号，以及显著形态（突破、头肩顶、双底等）。必须给出具体价格区间，但这些价格只能作为市场观察，不得作为买入、卖出、止损、止盈、建仓或减仓指令。

7. **新闻情绪分析**
   梳理[公司名]最近30天内最重要的新闻事件，综合评估当前市场情绪（正面/负面/中性），并分析最重要新闻可能引发的市场反应，不得确定性预测结果或建议采取投资动作。

8. **研究总结**
   综合以上基本面、风险、DCF情景分析、同行对标、催化剂、技术面及情绪分析，为[公司名]撰写一份平衡的研究总结。内容包括：（1）支持因素——可能支撑较强结果的主要因素，须包含不确定性说明；（2）风险因素——主要风险与不利因素；（3）关键不确定性——决定最终结果的2至3个核心变量。最后附一段简短的综合性结论段落。**不得给出买入/持有/卖出评级、目标价格，或任何直接/间接投资动作建议。** 所有分析结论须使用体现不确定性的措辞（如"表明"、"暗示"、"可能"、"基于当前数据"），不得使用"必然"、"保证"、"稳赚"等确定性语言。
