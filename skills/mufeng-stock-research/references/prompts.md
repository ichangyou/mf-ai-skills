# Stock Research Prompts (Bilingual)

These are the 8 canonical research prompts. Substitute `[Company]` with the target company name.

---

## English Prompts

1. **Fundamentals**
   Conduct a deep fundamental analysis of [Company], covering the latest financial statements (revenue, earnings, cash flow trends), competitive advantages (moat), and growth potential over the next 3–5 years.

2. **Downside Risk**
   Assess the downside risks for [Company], including market volatility exposure, key regulatory risks (antitrust, data privacy, sector-specific), and the impact of an economic recession or macro downturn on the business.

3. **DCF Valuation**
   Build a DCF (Discounted Cash Flow) model for [Company] to estimate intrinsic value per share. Explicitly state all assumptions: revenue growth rate (by year), operating margin, WACC, terminal growth rate, and shares outstanding. Show the sensitivity table for ±1% changes in WACC and terminal growth.

4. **Peer Benchmarking**
   Benchmark [Company] against its top 3–5 sector peers on key valuation multiples (P/E, Forward P/E, EV/EBITDA, P/S) and operational metrics (gross margin, operating margin, revenue growth, ROE). Explain whether [Company] deserves a premium or discount vs. peers.

5. **Upcoming Catalysts**
   Identify the top potential catalysts for [Company] over the next 6–12 months. Include product launches, earnings dates, M&A activity, regulatory approvals, macro/geopolitical events, and any analyst estimate revisions that could move the stock.

6. **Technical Analysis**
   Analyze [Company]'s price chart. Identify: current trend (bullish/bearish/sideways), key support levels, key resistance levels, volume signals, and any notable chart patterns (breakout, head & shoulders, double bottom, etc.). State specific price levels.

7. **News Sentiment**
   Summarize the most significant recent news (last 30 days) about [Company]. Assess the overall market sentiment (positive/negative/mixed), and explain the expected near-term price impact of the most important news items.

8. **Research Summary**
   Based on the fundamental analysis, risk assessment, DCF valuation, peer comparison, catalyst outlook, technical picture, and sentiment above, synthesize the key findings for [Company] into a balanced research summary. Present: (1) a Bull Case — the most important factors that could support the thesis, with appropriate uncertainty caveats; (2) a Bear Case — the most important risks and headwinds; (3) Key Uncertainties — the 2–3 variables that most determine the outcome. Conclude with a brief, balanced synthesis paragraph. Do not issue a Buy/Hold/Sell rating, a price target, or any direct investment instruction. All conclusions must include language reflecting inherent uncertainty (e.g., "suggests", "indicates", "may", "based on current data").

## 中文提示词

1. **基本面分析**
   对[公司名]进行基本面深度分析，包括最新财务报表（营收、盈利、现金流趋势）、核心竞争优势（护城河），以及未来3至5年的成长潜力。

2. **下行风险评估**
   评估[公司名]的下行风险，涵盖市场波动敞口、关键监管风险（反垄断、数据隐私、行业监管）以及经济衰退或宏观下行对业务的影响。

3. **DCF估值**
   使用DCF（现金流折现）模型计算[公司名]的每股内在价值，并详细说明所有假设：各年营收增长率、营业利润率、WACC（加权平均资本成本）、永续增长率及总股本。列出WACC和永续增长率各±1%变化下的敏感性分析表。

4. **同行对标**
   将[公司名]与行业前3至5家主要竞争对手在核心估值倍数（市盈率P/E、远期市盈率、EV/EBITDA、市销率P/S）和运营指标（毛利率、营业利润率、营收增速、ROE）上进行对比，并说明[公司名]相对同行应享受溢价还是折价。

5. **潜在催化剂**
   梳理[公司名]未来6至12个月的主要潜在催化剂，包括新产品发布、财报日期、并购动向、监管审批、宏观/地缘政治事件，以及可能影响股价的分析师预期调整。

6. **技术图表分析**
   分析[公司名]的价格走势图，识别：当前趋势方向（上涨/下跌/震荡）、关键支撑位、关键阻力位、成交量信号，以及显著形态（突破、头肩顶、双底等）。必须给出具体价格区间。

7. **新闻情绪分析**
   梳理[公司名]最近30天内最重要的新闻事件，综合评估当前市场情绪（正面/负面/中性），并分析最重要新闻对短期股价的预期影响。

8. **研究总结**
   综合以上基本面、风险、DCF估值、同行对标、催化剂、技术面及情绪分析，为[公司名]撰写一份平衡的研究总结。内容包括：（1）多头观点——支持投资论点的主要因素，须包含不确定性说明；（2）空头观点——主要风险与不利因素；（3）关键不确定性——决定最终结果的2至3个核心变量。最后附一段简短的综合性结论段落。**不得给出买入/持有/卖出评级、目标价格或任何直接投资建议。** 所有分析结论须使用体现不确定性的措辞（如"表明"、"暗示"、"可能"、"基于当前数据"），不得使用"必然"、"保证"、"稳赚"等确定性语言。
